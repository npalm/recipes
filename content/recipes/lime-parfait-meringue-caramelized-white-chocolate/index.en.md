An elegant dessert with ice-cold lime parfait, crispy meringue, and caramelized white chocolate. The fruit completes the dish.

## Components

### Lime Parfait
prepTime: 20
cookTime: 10
waitTime: 360

The creamy and ice-cold heart of your dessert.

#### Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks (medium size) {scale}
- 50 g granulated sugar {scale}
- 160 ml heavy cream (minimum 35% fat, cold) {scale}
- 45 ml lime juice (about 1.5 limes) {scale}
- 1 lime, zest {scale}
- Pinch of salt

#### Instructions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with {{50 g}} sugar and a pinch of salt in a double boiler until thick and pale (70째C - 75째C).

2. Continue whisking off the heat until the mixture is lukewarm.

3. Fold in the {{45 ml}} lime juice and zest.

4. Whip the {{160 ml}} heavy cream to soft peaks (thick like yogurt) and gently fold into the egg mixture.

5. Pour into silicone molds and freeze for at least 6 hours.

### Meringue Shards
prepTime: 10
cookTime: 120
waitTime: 30

For height and crispy, sweet crunch.

#### Ingredients

- 1 egg white (about 30-35 g) {scale}
- 50 g granulated sugar {scale}
- 25 g powdered sugar (optional, for extra crispness) {scale}
- A little lime zest

#### Instructions

1. Beat the egg white with the {{50 g}} granulated sugar until stiff, glossy peaks form.

2. Fold in the {{25 g}} powdered sugar and a little lime zest.

3. Spread very thin ({{2 mm}}) on parchment paper.

4. Dry in the oven at 90째C for 1.5 to 2 hours with the door slightly ajar.

5. Let cool completely and break into shards.

### Caramelized White Chocolate & Pistachio
prepTime: 10
cookTime: 10
waitTime: 15

The culinary surprise: nutty with the flavor of caramel.

#### Ingredients

- 75 g white chocolate, chopped {scale}
- 25 g unsalted pistachios {scale}

#### Instructions

1. Bake the chocolate pieces on parchment paper in the oven at 150째C for 5-10 minutes until golden brown.

2. Let cool and chop into coarse crumbs.

3. Chop the pistachios finely and mix them with the chocolate crumble.

### Fruit Duo (Pear & Persimmon)
prepTime: 15

The fresh counterpoint that completes the dish.

#### Ingredients

- 1 firm pear (e.g., Conference) {scale}
- 1 ripe persimmon (Sharon fruit) {scale}
- A drop of lime juice
- A little sugar

#### Instructions

1. Pear: Peel the pear and cut into very small cubes (brunoise). Mix with a drop of lime juice and a little sugar to prevent browning.

2. Persimmon: Cut the persimmon into thin, elegant slices (carpaccio-style). Use raw.

## Plating

Remove the lime parfait from the mold and place on the plate. Arrange the meringue shards around it, sprinkle the chocolate-pistachio crumble over it, add the pear brunoise and persimmon slices.

## Notes

- Many components can be made ahead of time
- Parfait and meringue can be made in parallel (parfait freezes while meringue dries in the oven)
- Cut fruit just before serving for optimal freshness
- Meringue can be made several days ahead and stored in an airtight container
- Ensure all equipment for the meringue is grease-free for best results
